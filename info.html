About Us

Jackson McAvoy and Naomy Chepngeno Chesengeny founded Favtune way back in September 2023. Although they barely knew each other, it was both of their lifelong dreams to create a unique, stylized experience for rating songs and sharing ratings with friends. Through chance alone, they were tasked as partners for a classroom project. It was then that they came together and created Favtune. Since then, they have ammased over 6 users, paving their way into the industry.
Our mission is to optimize your music rating experience. With Favtune, you can explore a vast collection of songs, rate them, and keep your music library organized. Whether you're a music aficionado or just looking for new tracks to groove to, we believe that music brings people together, and Favtune is here to enhance your musical journey.

This site was designed and published as part of the COMP333 Software Engineering class at Wesleyan University. This is a training exercise.
